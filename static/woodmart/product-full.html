Aurora Haze is a luminous, ethereal fragrance that captures the serene beauty of dawn. Opening with delicate citrus and dewy florals, it unfolds into a heart of soft amber and blooming jasmine, evoking the first golden rays of sunlight. A warm base of creamy sandalwood and subtle musk lingers on the skin, wrapping you in a gentle, radiant embrace. Elegant and uplifting, Aurora Haze is a scent that whispers of new beginnings and endless possibilities.

Top Notes:

Bergamot, Pimento Berry, Oregano

Heart Notes:

Amber, Citrus, Frankincense, Vanilla

Base Notes:

Oud, Leather, Patchouli, Sandalwood
